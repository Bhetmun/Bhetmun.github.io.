Task 1

The Pudding - visual essay website

Audience
  • The specific one that I looked at covered the topic of spotify music charts. It is clear the audience are music analysts who are specifically interested in the topic given that it is very minimalistic and straight to the point, but the audience age group is still somewhat young which we can tell from their use of emojis throughout the website
  • The range and depth of the material is appropriate, the ones interested in this are probably not too professional but still have an understanding of the meaning of statistics. The visuals that they do provide such as graphs and clickable music icons are very well suited for the audience as they are also users that are looking to expand their music library
  • There is plenty of interactivity with the website, you navigate the pages by clicking or pressing arrow keys and on several slides you can click on boxes to play the music of the artists that they are referencing
Signifiers
  • The purpose of the website is to provide and idea on how the most popular genres of music has changed over the years on spotify, but the secondary purpose is to encourage users to explore new music and not just what is trending.
  • The project is very easy to navigate given that you only need to click and the website will simply show you the next page, it is also has an intuitive 'going back' system where you can click the bar at the top of the page to go back to a previous page
Visuals
  • Makes good use of color since it uses neutral colours which does not stand out and is still pleasant to the eyes for a background
  • uses bright green for the interactive elements of the website, a nice touch since they are writing about spotify which has a green logo
  • The graphics and information is constantly changing layout but it is always laid out in a clear way that is easy to read and simple to follow
Content Design
  • Relevant to target audience, pays more attention to mention artists who are trending more which leans more towards the target audience as those artists relate more to a general audience
  • The content mentions several sources throughout the website including from the artists that they reference themselves, this makes the website quite credible.
